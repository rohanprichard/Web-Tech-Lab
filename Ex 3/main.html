Our Goals

Our aim is to provide our members with the best technical experience in their field of study in an international arena and promote multicultural and multi-linguistic exchange of ideas on a global scale. Our Motto : Work. Experience. Discover. The International Association for the Exchange of Students for Technical Experience, commonly referred to as IAESTE, is a non-profit, international organization that serves as a platform for exchanging students for technical work experience abroad. IAESTE provides paid, technical internships ranging from 4-52 weeks to students who are currently enrolled in a recognized university or college. IAESTE India a is a full member of the IAESTE network which encompasses more than 80 countries worldwide.
